Guest Talk on “Math-The Great way of Future”

August 26th, 2019
The Department of Mathematics, School of Applied Sciences, REVA University successfully organized Expert Lecture on 26th August,2019 The Resource person is Manan Khurma, Founder of Cuemath, an Edu Tech company funded by Google.  He has been teaching math for the last 15+ years, since I was a second year student at IIT Delhi. Over this duration, he has taught more than 10,000 students across all grades and many different settings. Since childhood, he loved math, and he believes every child has the ability to master this subject, if exposed to it properly.
Also, in today's world, every child needs a strong math foundation, because the future will be heavily skewed in favour of mathematical thinkers. Therefore, through Cuemath, he has dedicated himself to the mission of making every child great at math. 
  • Cuemath is India's largest math learning program
  • As of June 2019, we have a network of 4000 teachers teaching 40000 students across India using the Cuemath learning system
  • Cuemath is backed by top VC partners like Sequoia Capital and Google Capital (now CapitalG)
He has created lot of enthusiasm in the students to understand the Basic concepts in Math. Students were inspired by his Talk The event ended with vote of thanks by the Coordinator, Dr. Harsih Babu.G.A.
